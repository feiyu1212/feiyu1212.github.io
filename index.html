About Me

I am a first-year PhD student in the Data Science and Analytics Thrust at The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (Guangzhou).

My research focuses on the intersection of Medical AI and Multimodal Learning. I am particularly interested in developing progressive frameworks for spatial transcriptomics and medical image representation. Previously, I worked as an Algorithm Engineer at Samsung.
